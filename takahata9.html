stair-climbing mechanism

Stair climbing mechanism in low-friction environments

A new 8-wheel mechanism was designed to ascend and descend staircases even under low-friction conditions. We evaluated the mechanism on a prototype staircase with a riser of 150 mm and a tread of 320 mm.

Publications

  1. Tomoyuki Takahata and Tatsuya Harada, “Stair climbing mechanism aimed for use in low-friction environments,” The 40th annual conference of the Robotics Society of Japan (RSJ2022), 4H1-02, 2022. (Japanese domestic conference)
